### What this error really means (in your context)

Your wallet is rejecting the swap because the transaction being built (or gas-estimated) is coming out **above the chain’s per-tx gas cap** (“exceeds max transaction gas limit”). In practice this usually happens when:

* The app **sets/overrides gas incorrectly** (e.g., hardcoded `gas`, `gasLimit`, or an absurd multiplier).
* The app tries to call the wrong contract / wrong calldata (so simulation fails and gas estimate becomes nonsensical).
* The pool isn’t initialized / liquidity isn’t seeded, causing revert-ish behavior during estimation.
* Hook params or pool key fields are malformed (bad `hooks` address, wrong `tickSpacing`, wrong `fee`, wrong currency ordering), which makes the call extremely expensive or fails estimation.
* The app is calling **PoolManager.swap directly** when it should use the correct router / settled flow for your current setup (depends on your architecture), and the call fails in simulation.

So the updated prompt must include: **pre-swap simulation + sane gas estimation + no manual gas overrides + prevent sending a tx when simulation fails**.
